# Product

## Register

product

## Users

IELTS candidates preparing for exam modules under time pressure, often returning repeatedly to practice, review progress, and understand what to improve next.

## Product Purpose

IELTS Anywhere helps learners practice IELTS Reading, Listening, Writing, and Speaking, review results, build vocabulary and grammar skills, and move toward a target band with focused feedback.

## Brand Personality

Determined, concentrated, premium, and modern. The product should feel serious about progress without feeling cold or intimidating.

## Anti-references

Avoid anything that looks obviously AI-generated: generic SaaS layouts, decorative filler, cluttered dashboards, childish test-prep visuals, and overused purple gradients without purpose.

## Design Principles

- Keep practice momentum high: every screen should make the next useful action obvious.
- Make progress feel earned: use clear hierarchy, feedback, and status instead of decorative motivation.
- Preserve focus: reduce visual noise around forms, tests, and reports.
- Feel premium through restraint: polish spacing, typography, and interaction states rather than adding ornament.
- Design for repeat use: prioritize scannability, predictable navigation, and durable components.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-accessible flows. Support reduced motion, avoid color-only meaning, and keep form errors clear for non-native English speakers.
